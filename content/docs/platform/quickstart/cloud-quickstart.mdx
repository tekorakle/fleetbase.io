# Cloud Quickstart

Fleetbase Cloud is the fastest way to get started. Your environment is provisioned and pre-configured during signup — no infrastructure or setup required. This guide walks you through what to expect and what to do first once you're in.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create your account

    Go to [console.fleetbase.io](https://console.fleetbase.io/onboard) and fill in your name, email, password, organisation name, phone number, and country. Your organisation is created at this step — there's no separate setup afterwards.

    You'll receive a 6-digit verification code by email. Enter it to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Configure your workspace

    A short wizard collects your company size, industry, operational goals, and preferred integrations. This personalises your console — setting your dashboard layout, recommended integrations, and available presets before you ever log in. You can also add your first driver and vehicle here if you have them handy.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Start your 7-day trial

    Select a plan and complete checkout to activate your trial. A payment method is required — your card won't be charged until the 7 days are up, and you can cancel any time from billing settings.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete (or skip) the guided tour

    When you first land in the console, a guided tour starts automatically. It walks you through the interface and then takes you through creating your first driver and dispatching your first order. Complete it to get real data into the system, or skip it — it's available any time from **Help & Tours** in the console menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First things to do

Once you're in the console, these are the three most useful things to get done:

- **[Create an API Key](/docs/platform/developer-console/api-keys)** — Go to **Developer Console → API Keys** and generate your first key. You'll need this to connect any external system, app, or integration to your Fleetbase instance.

- **[Add your first driver and get Navigator](/docs/fleet-ops/drivers)** — Go to **Fleet-Ops → Drivers** to add your driver roster. Each driver uses the **Fleetbase Navigator** mobile app to receive orders, update status, and submit proof of delivery — share the download link from the driver profile.

- **[Create your first order](/docs/fleet-ops/orders)** — Go to **Fleet-Ops → Orders** and dispatch a delivery. Assign it to a driver and watch it move through the status lifecycle on the live map in real time.

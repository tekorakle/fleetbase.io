# Wallets

A **Wallet** is a digital account used to track the financial balance of a specific entity within your Fleetbase ecosystem.

Wallets are the core mechanism for managing payouts, tracking earnings, and handling internal transfers.

## Who Needs a Wallet?

Wallets can be assigned to almost any entity in Fleetbase:

- **Drivers**: To track their earnings from completed deliveries.
- **Vendors/Merchants**: To track their revenue from sales in a [Storefront](/docs/storefront) marketplace.
- **Customers**: To hold store credit, refunds, or prepaid balances.
- **Your Organization**: To track your platform's overall revenue and commissions.

## Creating a Wallet

Wallets are often created automatically. For example, when you onboard a new driver in [FleetOps](/docs/fleetops), you can configure the system to automatically generate a Wallet for them.

To manually create a Wallet:

1. Navigate to **Ledger** > **Wallets** in the Fleetbase Console.
2. Click **New Wallet**.
3. Select the **Owner** of the wallet (e.g., a specific Driver, Vendor, or Customer).
4. Select the **Currency** for the wallet. *Note: A single entity can have multiple wallets if they operate in multiple currencies.*
5. Click **Save**.

## Managing Balances

The balance of a Wallet is determined by the sum of all its associated [Transactions](/docs/ledger/payments/transactions).

You can manually adjust a Wallet's balance directly from the Console:

### Top-Up (Credit)
Adding funds to a wallet. This is often used to issue a refund to a customer as store credit, or to manually correct a driver's earnings.

### Payout (Debit)
Removing funds from a wallet. This is used when you actually transfer money to a driver's or vendor's real-world bank account. Recording a Payout in Ledger ensures their digital balance drops to zero, reflecting that they have been paid.

### Transfer
Moving funds from one Wallet to another. For example, transferring a commission fee from a Vendor's Wallet to your Organization's Wallet.

## Freezing a Wallet

If you suspect fraudulent activity, or if a vendor's account is suspended, you can **Freeze** their Wallet.

1. Open the Wallet in the Fleetbase Console.
2. Click the **Freeze** button.

When a Wallet is frozen, no new transactions can be processed against it. Funds cannot be added, and payouts cannot be issued until the Wallet is unfrozen by an administrator.

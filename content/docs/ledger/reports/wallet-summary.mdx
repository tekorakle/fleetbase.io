# Wallet Summary

The **Wallet Summary** report provides an analytical overview of all digital wallets in your Fleetbase ecosystem — total balances by wallet type, status breakdown, and top wallets by balance.

Navigate to **Ledger → Reports → Wallet Summary**.

![Wallet summary — balance by type pie chart, status breakdown, and top wallets by balance table](/images/placeholder.png)

## Report Sections

### Total Balances by Type

A breakdown of total wallet balances grouped by wallet type:

| Type | What it represents |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Driver wallets** | Total earnings held across all driver wallets |
| **Customer wallets** | Total prepaid credit across all customer wallets |
| **Company wallets** | Total balance in company/operating accounts |
| **User wallets** | Total balance in individual user accounts |

This tells you how much total cash your organization has committed to each stakeholder group — e.g., how much you owe your drivers in pending payouts.

### Status Breakdown

Wallet counts by status:
- **Active** — operational wallets
- **Frozen** — wallets currently blocked from debits
- **Closed** — inactive wallets

### Top Wallets by Balance

A ranked list of the wallets with the highest balances. Useful for identifying:
- Drivers with large pending earnings that need payout
- Customers with high prepaid credit
- Any anomalous balances that warrant review

### Summary Metrics

- **Total wallets** — count of all wallets
- **Total balance** — aggregate across all currencies and types
- **Active wallet count** — operational wallets
- **Average balance** — per-wallet average

## Currency

If you operate in multiple currencies, the wallet summary shows balances grouped by currency. There is no automatic conversion — each currency is reported separately.

## Using the Wallet Summary

- **Payroll/payout runs**: check driver wallet totals before initiating batch payouts
- **Float management**: monitor company wallet balances to ensure sufficient float for operations
- **Fraud detection**: large unusual balances in individual wallets may warrant investigation
- **Customer lifetime value**: customer wallet balances indicate customers who are heavily invested in your platform

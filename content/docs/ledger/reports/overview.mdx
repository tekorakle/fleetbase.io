# Financial Reports

The **Reports** module generates standard financial statements from your Ledger data. Reports are computed on-the-fly from journal entries — always current and accurate. No pre-computation required.

Navigate to **Ledger → Reports** to access all reports.

![Reports section — report type cards with descriptions and date range selectors](/images/placeholder.png)

## Available Reports

| Report | What it shows |
| :--- | :--- |
| [Balance Sheet](/docs/ledger/reports/balance-sheet) | Assets, liabilities, and equity at a point in time |
| [Income Statement](/docs/ledger/reports/income-statement) | Revenue, expenses, and net income for a period |
| [Cash Flow Statement](/docs/ledger/reports/cash-flow) | Operating, financing, and investing cash flows |
| [Trial Balance](/docs/ledger/reports/trial-balance) | All accounts with debit/credit totals — verifies books balance |
| [AR Aging](/docs/ledger/reports/ar-aging) | Outstanding invoices bucketed by how overdue they are |
| [Wallet Summary](/docs/ledger/reports/wallet-summary) | Wallet counts, total balances, and top wallets by balance |

## Dashboard KPIs

The **Ledger Dashboard** (home screen) shows key metrics at a glance:
- **Total Revenue** — sum of revenue account credits for the period (with year-over-year comparison)
- **Total Expenses** — sum of expense account debits
- **Net Income** — Revenue − Expenses
- **Outstanding AR** — total unpaid invoice balance
- **Overdue AR** — unpaid invoices past due date
- **Invoice Status Counts** — breakdown by status (draft, sent, paid, overdue)
- **Revenue Trend** — daily revenue bar chart
- **Recent Journal Entries** — last 10 entries

## Date Filtering

Most reports support a date range filter. Specify `start_date` and `end_date` to scope the report to a specific period (e.g., a financial quarter or fiscal year).

Default periods:
- Balance Sheet, Trial Balance: current date (point-in-time)
- Income Statement, Cash Flow: current month
- AR Aging: current date (outstanding invoices)
- Wallet Summary: all time

## How Reports Are Generated

All reports draw from the journal entries in your chart of accounts:

- **Balance Sheet**: aggregates asset, liability, and equity account balances as of a date
- **Income Statement**: sums revenue and expense accounts for a period
- **Cash Flow**: classifies transactions as operating, financing, or investing
- **Trial Balance**: lists all accounts with their total debits and credits
- **AR Aging**: groups open invoices by days past due date
- **Wallet Summary**: aggregates wallet balances by type and status

# Trial Balance

The **Trial Balance** lists all accounts in your chart of accounts with their total debit and credit activity for a period. Its primary purpose is to verify that total debits equal total credits — confirming that every journal entry was recorded correctly.

Navigate to **Ledger → Reports → Trial Balance**.

![Trial balance — account list with debit column, credit column, and totals row](/images/placeholder.png)

## Report Structure

| Column | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Account Code** | Account identifier from your chart of accounts |
| **Account Name** | Account name |
| **Type** | Asset, Liability, Equity, Revenue, or Expense |
| **Debit Total** | Sum of all debit entries for this account in the period |
| **Credit Total** | Sum of all credit entries for this account in the period |

At the bottom:
- **Total Debits** — sum of all debit totals across all accounts
- **Total Credits** — sum of all credit totals across all accounts

If the books are balanced: `Total Debits = Total Credits`

## Using the Trial Balance

A trial balance is typically run:

- **At period end** — before generating income statements and balance sheets
- **When discrepancies are suspected** — to locate the source of a reporting mismatch
- **For external audit preparation** — auditors often request a trial balance as the starting point

## When Debits ≠ Credits

If the totals do not match, there is a recording error. Common causes:
- A manual journal entry was entered with unequal debit and credit amounts
- A transaction was recorded without a corresponding journal entry
- A journal entry was partially reversed

Use the [General Ledger](/docs/ledger/accounting/general-ledger) to filter by specific accounts and dates to identify the discrepancy.

## Setting the Report Date

1. Navigate to **Ledger → Reports → Trial Balance**
2. Set the **As of Date** (balance reflects all entries up to end of that date)
3. Click **Generate**

For a period-specific trial balance, set both a start and end date to scope entries to that window.

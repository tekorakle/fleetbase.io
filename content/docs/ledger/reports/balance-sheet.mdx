# Balance Sheet

The **Balance Sheet** is a snapshot of your organization's financial position at a specific date. It answers the question: what does your organization own (assets) and what does it owe (liabilities and equity)?

Navigate to **Ledger → Reports → Balance Sheet**.

![Balance sheet — assets, liabilities, equity, totals row, balanced indicator](/images/screenshots/ledger/ledger-balance-sheet.webp)

## The Accounting Equation

A balance sheet always satisfies:

```
Assets = Liabilities + Equity
```

If your books are in balance, total assets will equal total liabilities plus equity. The report shows a **Balanced** indicator — green when the equation holds, red when it doesn't.

## Report Structure

### Assets

All **asset** accounts from your chart of accounts. Default seeded assets:

- Cash (`CASH-DEFAULT`)
- Bank Account (`BANK-DEFAULT`)
- Accounts Receivable (`AR-DEFAULT`)
- Stripe Clearing Account (`STRIPE-CLEARING`)
- Payment Gateway Clearing (`GATEWAY-CLEARING`)
- Prepaid Expenses (`PREPAID-DEFAULT`)

**Total Assets**

### Liabilities

All **liability** accounts:

- Accounts Payable (`AP-DEFAULT`)
- Wallet Liability Pool (`WALLET-POOL`) plus per-wallet `WALLET-{uuid}` accounts
- Driver Earnings Payable (`DRIVER-PAYABLE`)
- Customer Credit Liability (`CUSTOMER-CREDIT`)
- Tax Payable (`TAX-PAYABLE`)
- Stripe Fees Payable (`STRIPE-FEES-PAYABLE`)
- Deferred Revenue (`DEFERRED-REVENUE`)

**Total Liabilities**

### Equity

All **equity** accounts. Default seeded equity:

- Owner's Equity (`EQUITY-DEFAULT`)
- Retained Earnings (`RETAINED-EARNINGS`)

Net income for the current period is reflected in the income statement, not as a discrete equity account.

**Total Equity**

### Verification

The report also shows the combined total of liabilities and equity for direct comparison:

```
Total Assets = Total Liabilities + Total Equity
```

If these do not match, there is an accounting error — missing journal entries or an unbalanced entry.

## Setting the Report Date

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to **Ledger → Reports → Balance Sheet**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set the **As of Date** — the balance sheet reflects account balances at end of that date.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Generate**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The default is today.

## What Wallet Liabilities Mean

The **Wallet Liability Pool** plus the auto-created per-wallet liability accounts (`WALLET-{wallet_uuid}`) represent the total balance owed to all wallet holders. They increase when wallets are credited and decrease when payouts are made. On the balance sheet, this appears as a liability — your organization owes this amount to its wallet holders.

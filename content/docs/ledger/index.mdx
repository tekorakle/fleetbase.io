# Ledger

**Ledger** is the accounting and invoicing extension for Fleetbase.

It provides a centralized system for managing the financial aspects of your logistics or e-commerce operations, allowing you to track revenue, generate invoices, and manage payments.

## Core Capabilities

Ledger is designed to integrate deeply with both [FleetOps](/docs/fleetops) and [Storefront](/docs/storefront), automatically capturing financial data as transactions occur.

- **[Wallets](/docs/ledger/wallets)**: Digital accounts for tracking balances. You can create wallets for your organization, your drivers, or your vendors.
- **[Transactions](/docs/ledger/transactions)**: The fundamental record of money moving into, out of, or between Wallets.
- **[Invoices](/docs/ledger/invoices)**: Formal requests for payment sent to customers or generated by vendors.

## Integration with FleetOps

When you use Ledger alongside FleetOps, you can automate much of your billing process.

For example, when a driver completes a delivery, FleetOps can automatically generate a [Transaction](/docs/ledger/transactions) in Ledger, crediting the driver's [Wallet](/docs/ledger/wallets) with their earnings for that specific job.

Similarly, if you provide logistics services to B2B clients, you can use Ledger to automatically generate monthly [Invoices](/docs/ledger/invoices) based on the total number of orders fulfilled for that client.

## Integration with Storefront

When used with Storefront, Ledger acts as the financial system of record for your e-commerce sales.

When a customer completes a checkout, the payment is recorded as a Transaction in Ledger. If you operate a multi-vendor marketplace, Ledger can automatically split the payment, crediting the merchant's Wallet with their portion of the sale while retaining your platform commission in your organization's Wallet.

## Getting Started

To begin using Ledger, you must first install the extension via the Fleetbase CLI or the [Registry](/docs/platform/extensions).

Once installed, the Ledger module will appear in your Fleetbase Console sidebar, allowing you to begin configuring your first [Wallet](/docs/ledger/wallets).

# Ledger

**Ledger** is the accounting and invoicing extension for Fleetbase. It provides a complete financial management layer — double-entry bookkeeping, automated invoicing, digital wallets, payment gateway processing, and standard financial reporting — integrated directly with Fleet-Ops and Storefront.

![Ledger dashboard — KPI cards, revenue trend chart, recent journal entries](/images/placeholder.png)

## What Ledger Does

| Capability | What it covers |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Chart of Accounts** | Define asset, liability, equity, revenue, and expense accounts |
| **Double-Entry Bookkeeping** | Every transaction creates journal entries across two accounts |
| **Invoicing** | Create, send, and collect payment on invoices |
| **Invoice Templates** | Customize the look of your invoices with company branding |
| **Digital Wallets** | Track balances for drivers, customers, vendors, and your company |
| **Payment Gateways** | Stripe, QPay, and Cash — process payments and refunds |
| **Transactions** | Full audit trail for every financial event |
| **Financial Reports** | Balance sheet, income statement, cash flow, trial balance, AR aging, wallet summary |
| **Fleet-Ops Integration** | Auto-generate invoices when orders complete; credit driver wallets with earnings |
| **Storefront Integration** | Record payment transactions when checkouts complete; manage customer wallet credit |

## Key Concepts

- **Account** — a named ledger account with a type (asset, liability, equity, revenue, or expense) and a computed balance
- **Journal Entry** — a double-entry record that debits one account and credits another (every financial event creates exactly one journal entry)
- **Invoice** — a formal request for payment with line items, tax, and due date; lifecycle: draft → sent → viewed → partial/paid/overdue
- **Wallet** — a digital balance tied to a driver, customer, vendor, or company; supports deposit, withdrawal, transfer, top-up, and freeze
- **Transaction** — an immutable record of money moving into, out of, or between wallets
- **Gateway** — a payment processor (Stripe, QPay, Cash) with encrypted credentials and sandbox support

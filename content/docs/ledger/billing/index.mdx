# Billing & Invoicing

The **Billing** module in Ledger provides the tools necessary to generate, send, and manage invoices for your customers or B2B clients.

Whether you are billing a client for a month of logistics services in [FleetOps](/docs/fleetops) or generating a receipt for a wholesale order in [Storefront](/docs/storefront), Ledger acts as the central invoicing engine.

## Core Concepts

### [Invoices](/docs/ledger/billing/invoices)
An Invoice is a formal request for payment sent to a customer. It details the products or services provided, the quantities, the prices, and the total amount due.

### [Invoice Templates](/docs/ledger/billing/invoice-templates)
Invoice Templates allow you to customize the look and feel of your invoices. You can add your company logo, adjust the layout, and define default payment terms and notes.

## The Invoicing Lifecycle

A typical invoicing lifecycle in Fleetbase involves the following steps:

1. **Invoice Creation**: You create a new [Invoice](/docs/ledger/billing/invoices) in the Fleetbase Console, either manually or automatically via an integration with FleetOps or Storefront.
2. **Adding Line Items**: You add line items to the invoice, detailing the products or services provided.
3. **Sending the Invoice**: You send the invoice to the customer via email or a direct link.
4. **Payment Collection**: The customer pays the invoice, either online via a [Payment Gateway](/docs/ledger/payments/gateways) or offline (e.g., via bank transfer or check).
5. **Recording Payment**: Once payment is received, you record the payment against the invoice in Ledger. This automatically generates a `payment` [Transaction](/docs/ledger/payments/transactions) and updates the invoice status to `paid`.

## Automated Invoicing

While you can manually create invoices, Ledger is designed to automate the billing process wherever possible.

### FleetOps Integration
If you provide logistics services to B2B clients, you can configure FleetOps to automatically generate monthly invoices based on the total number of [Orders](/docs/fleetops/operations/orders) fulfilled for that client.

### Storefront Integration
When a customer completes a wholesale order in Storefront, the system can automatically generate an invoice and send it to the customer, rather than requiring immediate payment via credit card.

# Pricing

Storefront provides a flexible pricing engine that allows you to configure simple flat rates, complex variant pricing, and dynamic scheduled pricing.

## Base Pricing

The simplest form of pricing is the **Base Price**. This is the default price of a [Product](/docs/storefront/catalog/products) if no variants or special rules apply.

You set the Base Price when creating or editing a product in the Fleetbase Console.

The currency used for the Base Price is determined by the **Currency** setting of the [Store](/docs/storefront/stores/stores-and-networks) that owns the product.

## Variant Pricing

If a product has [Variants](/docs/storefront/catalog/variants-and-addons) (e.g., Size: Small, Medium, Large), the Base Price is often overridden by the Variant Price.

When you create a Variant, you assign it a specific price.

When a customer selects that Variant in your frontend application, the [Storefront API](/docs/api-reference/storefront) will use the Variant Price instead of the Base Price to calculate the cart total.

## Add-on Pricing

[Add-ons](/docs/storefront/catalog/variants-and-addons) (e.g., "Extra Cheese") have their own specific prices.

When a customer selects an Add-on, its price is added to the Base Price (or Variant Price) of the product.

For example:
- Base Product: Cheeseburger ($10.00)
- Add-on: Extra Cheese (+$1.00)
- Total Price: $11.00

## Scheduled Pricing (Sales & Happy Hours)

Storefront allows you to schedule temporary price changes for specific products. This is useful for running sales, promotions, or "Happy Hour" specials.

To schedule a price change:

1. Navigate to **Storefront** > **Products** in the Fleetbase Console.
2. Open the product you want to modify.
3. Go to the **Pricing** tab.
4. Click **Add Scheduled Price**.
5. Provide the details:
   - **Price**: The new, temporary price.
   - **Start Date/Time**: When the new price should take effect.
   - **End Date/Time**: When the price should revert to the Base Price.
6. Click **Save**.

During the scheduled window, the [Storefront API](/docs/api-reference/storefront) will automatically use the Scheduled Price instead of the Base Price. Once the window expires, the price will automatically revert.

*Note: Scheduled Pricing overrides the Base Price, but it does not override Variant or Add-on prices. If you need to discount a specific Variant, you must create a Scheduled Price specifically for that Variant.*

# Location Settings

**Location Settings** covers the global delivery and location configuration for your store. Individual store locations (addresses, hours) are managed per-store under **Storefront → [Store] → Locations**.

Navigate to **Storefront → Settings → Locations** for store-wide location defaults.

## Default Service Area

You can link your store to a Fleet-Ops **Service Area** to define the geographic region where you accept orders. Customers outside this area will be unable to complete checkout.

To set the default service area:

1. Navigate to **Storefront → Settings → Locations**
2. Select a **Service Area** from the dropdown (service areas are created in **Fleet-Ops → Resources → Service Areas**)
3. Click **Save**

Individual store locations can override this default by assigning their own zone.

## Multiple Store Locations

If your store has multiple physical locations, Storefront automatically routes each order to the nearest open location based on the customer's delivery address. See [Store Locations](/docs/storefront/stores/store-locations) for adding and managing individual locations.

## Delivery Radius

The default delivery radius is inherited from Fleet-Ops routing settings. To set a custom radius for your store:

1. Configure the service area boundary in **Fleet-Ops → Resources → Service Areas**
2. Assign the service area to your store location

Orders from outside the configured boundary will be declined at checkout with a delivery-unavailable message.

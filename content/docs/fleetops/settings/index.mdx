# Settings

The **Settings** section is where you configure FleetOps to match the specific operational requirements of your business.

Because Fleetbase is designed to be highly adaptable, almost every aspect of the dispatch and management workflow can be customized here.

## Core Configuration Areas

### [Navigator App](/docs/fleetops/settings/navigator-app)
Control the experience for your drivers. Here you can configure what information drivers see on their mobile devices, what actions they are required to take (e.g., mandatory proof of delivery), and how the app tracks their location.

### [Routing](/docs/fleetops/settings/routing)
Configure the algorithms that power the [Routing & Dispatching](/docs/fleetops/operations/routing-and-dispatching) engine. Set your preferred routing provider (e.g., Google Maps, Mapbox, OSRM), define default vehicle speeds, and set rules for automated batching and dispatching.

### [Scheduling](/docs/fleetops/settings/scheduling)
Set the default working hours for your fleet, define standard shift lengths, and configure how the [Scheduler](/docs/fleetops/operations/scheduler) handles conflicts and availability.

### [Payments](/docs/fleetops/settings/payments)
If you charge customers directly for deliveries (e.g., via the [Storefront](/docs/storefront) extension or a custom portal), configure your payment gateways (like Stripe or PayPal) here.

### [Notifications](/docs/fleetops/settings/notifications)
Set up automated SMS and email alerts for your customers and drivers. You can customize the templates for events like "Order Dispatched", "Driver Arriving", and "Delivery Completed".

### [Custom Fields](/docs/fleetops/settings/custom-fields)
Define the specific data points you need to collect for your operations. Custom fields can be attached to Orders, Vehicles, Drivers, or Contacts.

### [Orchestrator](/docs/fleetops/settings/orchestrator)
Manage the global settings for your automation rules, including default fallback actions if a rule fails to execute.

## Global Settings vs. Organization Settings

It's important to note that Fleetbase supports multi-tenancy via [Organizations](/docs/platform/iam/organizations-and-groups).

The settings configured in this section apply to the **current Organization** you are logged into. If you manage multiple distinct brands or regional subsidiaries within a single Fleetbase instance, you can configure different settings for each one.

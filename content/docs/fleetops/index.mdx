# FleetOps

**FleetOps** is the flagship extension for Fleetbase. It transforms the core platform into a comprehensive Transport Management System (TMS) and fleet dispatch solution.

Whether you are managing a small local delivery fleet, a nationwide long-haul operation, or an on-demand courier network, FleetOps provides the tools you need to manage drivers, vehicles, orders, and real-time tracking.

## Core Capabilities

FleetOps is divided into several key operational areas:

### [Management](/docs/fleetops/management)
The foundation of your operations. Manage your core resources, including:
- **Drivers**: Profiles, schedules, and compliance documents.
- **Vehicles**: Fleet inventory, specifications, and assignments.
- **Contacts & Vendors**: Customers, partners, and third-party logistics providers.
- **Places**: Warehouses, stores, and delivery locations.

### [Operations](/docs/fleetops/operations)
The day-to-day execution of your logistics network.
- **Orders**: Create, dispatch, and track deliveries.
- **Service Rates**: Define pricing models based on distance, weight, or custom zones.
- **Zones & Service Areas**: Geofence your operational regions.

### [Connectivity](/docs/fleetops/connectivity)
Integrate your fleet with external systems.
- **Integrated Vendors**: Connect with third-party delivery networks (e.g., Lalamove, Stuart) to overflow orders when your own fleet is at capacity.
- **Networks**: Manage complex multi-tenant logistics networks.

### [Maintenance](/docs/fleetops/maintenance)
Keep your fleet on the road.
- **Issues**: Track vehicle defects and driver reports.
- **Fuel Reports**: Monitor fuel consumption and expenses.

### [Analytics](/docs/fleetops/analytics)
Gain insights into your performance.
- **Dashboards**: Real-time operational metrics.
- **Reports**: Historical data on delivery times, driver performance, and costs.

## The Navigator App

FleetOps is designed to work seamlessly with the **Navigator** mobile app.

When an order is dispatched in the FleetOps console, it is instantly pushed to the assigned driver's Navigator app. The app provides the driver with turn-by-turn navigation, proof of delivery capture (signatures, photos), and real-time location tracking that feeds back into the FleetOps console.

To learn more about the driver experience, see the [Navigator App](/docs/apps/navigator-app) documentation.

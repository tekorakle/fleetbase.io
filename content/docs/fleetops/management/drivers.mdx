# Drivers

Drivers are the core workforce of your logistics operation. In FleetOps, a Driver profile represents the individual who will be executing deliveries, updating order statuses, and interacting with customers via the Navigator mobile app.

## Creating a Driver

To add a new driver to your fleet:

1. Navigate to **FleetOps** > **Drivers** in the Fleetbase Console.
2. Click the **New Driver** button.
3. Fill in the driver's details:
   - **Name**: The driver's full name.
   - **Phone Number**: Essential for SMS notifications and customer contact.
   - **Email**: Used for logging into the Navigator app.
   - **Internal ID**: An optional custom identifier (e.g., an employee ID number).
   - **Status**: Set the initial status (e.g., `Active`, `Inactive`).
4. Click **Save**.

Once created, the driver will receive an email or SMS invitation to download the Navigator app and set their password.

## Managing Driver Profiles

Clicking on a driver in the list opens their detailed profile, where you can manage their assignments, documents, and activity.

### Assigning a Vehicle

A driver must be assigned to a vehicle to execute deliveries.

1. In the driver's profile, locate the **Vehicle** section.
2. Click **Assign Vehicle**.
3. Select an available vehicle from your fleet inventory.
4. Click **Save**.

The driver's current location and status will now be associated with that vehicle.

### Managing Documents

Compliance is critical in logistics. You can upload and track important documents directly on the driver's profile.

1. In the driver's profile, navigate to the **Documents** tab.
2. Click **Upload Document**.
3. Select the file (e.g., a scan of their driver's license or insurance certificate).
4. Provide a name and optional expiration date for the document.

If a document has an expiration date, FleetOps can be configured to send automated [Notifications](/docs/platform/features/notifications) before it expires.

## Driver Status and Availability

A driver's status indicates their current operational state. This status is updated automatically based on their activity in the Navigator app, but can also be changed manually by a dispatcher.

Common statuses include:
- **Offline**: The driver is not currently working.
- **Online**: The driver is on shift and available for dispatch.
- **Active**: The driver is currently executing an order.

### Tracking Location

When a driver is `Online` or `Active`, the Navigator app continuously transmits their GPS location back to FleetOps. This allows dispatchers to see the driver's real-time position on the map and calculate accurate ETAs for active orders.

## Driver Performance

The driver profile also provides a summary of their historical performance, including:
- Total orders completed.
- Average delivery time.
- Customer ratings (if enabled).
- Recent activity logs.

This data is invaluable for identifying top performers and addressing operational bottlenecks.

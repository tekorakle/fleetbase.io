# Licensing

Fleetbase uses a dual-licensing model to support both the open-source community and commercial enterprises.

## Open-Source License (AGPL-3.0)

The core Fleetbase platform, including the API, Console, and official extensions (FleetOps, Storefront, Ledger), is released under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)**.

### What this means for you:
- **Free to use**: You can download, install, and use Fleetbase for free, even for commercial purposes.
- **Free to modify**: You can modify the source code to suit your needs.
- **Copyleft requirement**: If you modify the Fleetbase source code and make it available over a network (e.g., as a SaaS product or a public-facing application), you **must** make your modified source code available to your users under the same AGPL-3.0 license.

The AGPL-3.0 license is designed to ensure that improvements to the core platform are shared back with the community.

[Read the full AGPL-3.0 License text &rarr;](https://github.com/fleetbase/fleetbase/blob/master/LICENSE)

## Commercial License

If your organization requires a license without the AGPL-3.0 copyleft requirements, we offer a **Commercial License**.

### Why choose a Commercial License?
- **Keep your code private**: You can modify Fleetbase and integrate it into your proprietary, closed-source products without being required to release your source code.
- **White-labeling**: Remove Fleetbase branding and logos from the Console and mobile apps.
- **Premium Support**: Get direct access to the Fleetbase core team for priority support, architecture reviews, and bug fixes.
- **Indemnification**: Legal protection and warranties for enterprise use.

## Mobile Apps Licensing

The open-source mobile apps (Navigator App and Storefront App) are typically released under the **MIT License**, which is highly permissive and allows you to white-label, modify, and distribute the apps in the App Store and Google Play Store without releasing your source code.

Please check the `LICENSE` file in the respective GitHub repositories to confirm the exact license for each app.

# Management

The **Management** section of FleetOps is where you define and maintain the core resources that power your logistics network. Before you can dispatch an order or track a delivery, you need to set up the entities that will perform the work.

This section covers the creation, configuration, and ongoing management of your fleet's assets and partners.

## Core Resources

### [Drivers](/docs/fleetops/management/drivers)
The individuals who execute your deliveries. Manage driver profiles, contact information, assigned vehicles, and compliance documents (like licenses and insurance).

### [Vehicles](/docs/fleetops/management/vehicles)
The physical assets in your fleet. Track vehicle specifications (make, model, year, license plate), current status, and assignment history.

### [Fleets](/docs/fleetops/management/fleets)
Group your drivers and vehicles into logical units. Fleets allow you to organize your resources by region, vehicle type, or operational purpose (e.g., "Downtown Bike Couriers", "Long-Haul Trucks").

### [Contacts](/docs/fleetops/management/contacts)
The people and organizations you interact with. Contacts can represent customers, recipients, or internal staff members who need to be associated with specific orders or locations.

### [Vendors](/docs/fleetops/management/vendors)
Third-party logistics providers or partner companies that you work with. Vendors can be assigned orders when your internal fleet is at capacity.

### [Places](/docs/fleetops/management/places)
The physical locations involved in your operations. Places can represent warehouses, retail stores, customer addresses, or specific pickup/dropoff points.

## Maintenance & Reporting

In addition to core resources, the Management section also includes tools for keeping your fleet running smoothly:

### [Issues](/docs/fleetops/management/issues)
Track and resolve operational problems. Drivers can report issues (like a flat tire or a delayed pickup) directly from the Navigator app, which are then logged and managed here.

### [Fuel Reports](/docs/fleetops/management/fuel-reports)
Monitor your fleet's fuel consumption and expenses. Drivers can submit fuel receipts via the app, allowing you to track costs per vehicle and identify inefficiencies.

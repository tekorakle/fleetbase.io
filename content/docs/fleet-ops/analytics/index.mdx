# Analytics

Data is only useful if you can understand it. The **Analytics** section of FleetOps transforms the raw data generated by your daily operations into actionable insights.

By analyzing trends in your order volume, driver performance, and maintenance costs, you can identify inefficiencies, optimize your resources, and ultimately improve your bottom line.

## The Analytics Dashboard

When you open the Analytics section in the Fleetbase Console, you are presented with a customizable dashboard.

This dashboard is built using the platform's core [Dashboards & Widgets](/docs/platform/features/dashboards-and-widgets) feature. It allows you to arrange various visual widgets (charts, graphs, and key metric callouts) to create a personalized view of your fleet's performance.

### Key Metrics

Common metrics tracked on the Analytics dashboard include:

- **Order Volume**: The total number of orders created, dispatched, and completed over a specific time period (e.g., today, this week, this month).
- **On-Time Delivery Rate**: The percentage of orders delivered before their scheduled ETA or SLA deadline.
- **Driver Utilization**: The percentage of time your drivers spend actively executing orders versus sitting idle.
- **Total Maintenance Cost**: The aggregated cost of all [Work Orders](/docs/fleetops/maintenance/work-orders) (parts and labor) completed within a given timeframe.
- **Fuel Efficiency**: The average fuel consumption across your fleet, calculated from [Fuel Reports](/docs/fleetops/management/fuel-reports) and distance traveled.

## Customizing Your View

You can customize the Analytics dashboard to focus on the metrics that matter most to your specific role:

- **Dispatchers** might focus on real-time metrics like active orders, unassigned orders, and average delivery time.
- **Fleet Managers** might focus on maintenance costs, vehicle utilization, and fuel efficiency.
- **Executives** might focus on high-level revenue metrics (generated from [Service Quotes](/docs/fleetops/operations/service-quotes) and completed orders) and overall on-time performance.

You can add, remove, and resize widgets to create the perfect layout.

## Deep Dives with Reports

While the dashboard provides a high-level overview, sometimes you need to dig deeper into the data.

For detailed analysis, FleetOps provides a robust [Reports](/docs/fleetops/analytics/reports) engine. Reports allow you to query your historical data, apply complex filters, and export the results for use in external tools like Excel or specialized BI software.
